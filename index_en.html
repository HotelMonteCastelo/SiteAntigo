Welcome

Hotel Monte Castelo is located in a residential street in the historic district of Glory, a few meters from Gloria subway station, with easy access to the main sights of the city and the financial center of Rio de Janeiro. The ideal option for those who are traveling for leisure or business.

THE HOTEL

Hotel Monte Castelo is a historic mansion built in 1928 with accommodation equipped with LED TV 32' appliances, air conditioning, internet access free high speed Wi-Fi, USB sockets, telephone, private bathroom, minibar and workstation. The hotel offers 24h reception. For the convenience of guests, the hotel offers easy access to bars, restaurants, supermarkets, banks and pharmacies.

LOCATION

Strategically located, 10 minutes from Santos Dumont airport, Hotel Monte Castelo is situated in the south of the city of Rio de Janeiro, between the financial center and the main sights of the city.

A short walk will take you to the “MAM” the Museum of Modern Art, the Municipal Theatre, Lapa "point" the center of the city’s night life and to the renovated Marina da Glória, the bucolic cultural stronghold of Santa Teresa, as well as being next to the largest concentration of embassies and consulates in the city.

The hotel is really close to the metro station providing quick access to the main beaches of the city, as well as to the Porto Maravilha, where the Museum of Tomorrow and the Rio MAR Art Museum are situated. It is also close to the Corcovado (The Christ the Redeemer Statue), Sugar Loaf and the Sambadrome, the site of the samba schools’ parades.

Our Mission

The purpose of the Hotel is to provide a stay where you feel like you are in your home, with all the comfort. It was the first hotel in Rio de Janeiro to have wired internet connection by cable in all its rooms, long time before the invention of WiFI internet connection. So it was the foreigners' favorite hotel in Rio de Janeiro. At this time, the Brazilians still did not own a Laptop. We were also the first hotel to provide a hotel business center with computers, at no cost, to its guests. We are also the only hotel in Rio de Janeiro offering a free library room to all its guests with hundreds of books of various literary genres.

The hotel is small with only 38 rooms. All rooms are equipped with telephone extension, cable TV with a large variety of channels, refrigerator, hot shower and air conditioning. Hygiene is a daily concern for us. With all these amenities, we seek the lowest daily value for our guests. For what we offer we can not lower our price anymore. To our guests all possible comfort at the lowest cost.

The establishment is the result of great effort since 2002 of its owners Valterson and Mercedes Botelho (see the picture below) and still continues being administered by the Botelho family.

RIO DE JANEIRO

Rio de Janeiro is the largest international tourist destination in Brazil, Latin America and the whole of the Southern Hemisphere, known as the “Cidade Maravilhosa” the Marvelous City. Part of the city was designated as a World Cultural Heritage site for Humanity named "Rio de Janeiro: Carioca Landscape between the Mountains and the Sea ", classified by UNESCO on 1 July 2012. It is one of the main economic, cultural and financial centers of the country, it is internationally known for several cultural and landscape icons such as Sugar Loaf Mountain, the Corcovado hill with the statue of Christ the Redeemer and the numerous beaches within the city.

It has been the state capital of Brazil (1621-1815), a colony of the Portuguese Empire, from 1763 to 1815, then the United Kingdom of Portugal, Brazil and the Algarves (1815-1822), the Empire of Brazil (1822-1889 ) and the Republic of the United States of Brazil (1889-1968) until 1960, when the seat of government was moved to the newly built Brasília.

The city hosted the soccer World Cup in 2014 and the Olympic Games in 2016. Rio de Janeiro has undergone many changes that today make it a city that offers the full package for those interested in: history, culture, fun, good music and beautiful beaches.
